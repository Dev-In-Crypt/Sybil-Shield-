// COOKIES

Tiny answer: we don't use tracking cookies. Slightly longer answer below.

// what_we_use

STRICTLY NECESSARY

Session cookies for authentication once you log into the dashboard. Without these, nothing works. No opt-out — they're functionally required.

  • · sybilshield_session — 7 days · HttpOnly · Secure · SameSite=Lax

PREFERENCES

Local-storage values for things like remembered dashboard view. Not transmitted to our servers. Clear browser data to remove.

  • · sybilshield_api_key — your API key (local only)
  • · sybilshield_theme — if/when we add light mode

// what_we_DONT_use

  • › No Google Analytics
  • › No Facebook pixel
  • › No retargeting cookies
  • › No third-party advertising
  • › No cross-site tracking

// future

We may add privacy-respecting analytics (Plausible, Fathom) to understand which pages help users. They use no cookies at all. We'll update this page if/when added.
