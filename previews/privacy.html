// legal

// PRIVACY

What we collect, why, how long we keep it, and how to get it deleted. Plain English.

// last updated 2026-05-24 · effective immediately upon revision

// 01 · who_we_are

SybilShield is an open-source Sybil-detection platform operated as an indie-developer project pre-incorporation. When we incorporate (planned Q4 2026), this notice will be updated with the legal entity name and address.

// 02 · what_we_collect

FROM CUSTOMERS

Email · optional name / company · IP of registration · API call logs (timestamp, endpoint, status). Used for billing, abuse prevention, and account recovery.

FROM ANALYSES

Wallet addresses you submit. They're public on-chain data, but treated as your confidential input — we do not republish them outside aggregate stats.

FROM APPEALS

Wallet address, reason text, optional contact email. Used to review the flag; never republished with personally identifying details.

PASSIVELY

Standard server access logs (IP, user-agent). Retained 30 days for security debugging, then aggregated and discarded.

// 03 · what_we_DONT_collect

  • › Wallet private keys · we never ask for them, period
  • › Browser fingerprints · no canvas/audio/font tracking
  • › Third-party tracking pixels · no Facebook/Google retargeting
  • › Cookies beyond strictly-necessary session ones

// 04 · how_long_we_keep

ACTIVE ACCOUNT

Until you delete · or 2 years of inactivity

ANALYSIS RESULTS

90 days by default · longer on paid plans · purged on request

AUDIT LOG

7 years (legal-defense requirement) · anonymised after account deletion

// 05 · who_sees_what

  • › You see everything about your account, analyses, and the addresses you submitted
  • › Other customers never see your data — period
  • › SybilShield ops have admin access for incident response only · access is logged
  • › Reviewers see appeal submissions for analyses they're assigned · names redacted
  • › Public sees only aggregate statistics published in blog posts (no individual addresses)

// 06 · your_rights

You can: download all your data, correct mistakes, delete your account (which deletes or anonymises everything we have about you), object to specific uses, and complain to a data-protection authority.

To exercise: email support@sybilshield.org. We respond within 30 days.

// 07 · subprocessors

Service providers who process data on our behalf:

  • › Hosting — Railway / Vercel / Supabase / Upstash (will be confirmed at production launch)
  • › On-chain data — Alchemy (for tx ingestion) · PublicNode RPC (for derive scripts)
  • › Email — provider TBD at production launch
  • › Payments — NowPayments (crypto) · Stripe (cards, post-incorporation)

// 08 · changes

We'll announce material changes here at least 14 days before they take effect. Email all customers if changes are material.
