Chapter 02

Six independent methods

No single signal is enough to convict. Six methods running in parallel produce convergent evidence — a verdict the community can audit, not a black-box score.

01.

Funding source

Cluster wallets sharing a non-exchange funder within a tight time window. Catches the majority of amateur farms.

02.

Behavioural clustering

Density-based grouping on transaction-pattern features. Mechanical sequences surface immediately.

03.

Graph community detection

Leiden algorithm on the in-batch transaction graph. Dense isolated subgraphs are farms.

04.

Temporal anomaly

Hour-of-day entropy, inter-transaction autocorrelation, dormant-burst patterns. Humans are messy.

05.

Cross-chain identity linking

Deterministic linking via bridge events. Eight chains supported today.

06.

ML ensemble scoring

LightGBM weighted by label confidence tier. Honest evaluation on T1+G1 holdout only.

Chapter 03

Our editorial principles

Decisions that matter, made transparent.

Evidence before verdict

No flagged address goes out without a traceable evidence array. Every signal points at the specific feature or cluster that produced it.

Appeals are first-class

Anyone can dispute a score without an account. 48-hour response policy. Immutable audit log for every event, including reversals.

Open methodology

Algorithms, feature extractors, audit-log schema, and appeal protocol live under MIT on GitHub. Your community can audit our claims.
