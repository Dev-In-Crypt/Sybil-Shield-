02 · Detection stack

Six methods. One verdict.

Independent signals so no single method is gameable. Every score traces back to evidence.

01

Funding source

Wallets funded by the same source within a tight window cluster as one entity. Catches the majority of amateur farms.

02

Behavioral (HDBSCAN)

Tx-pattern density clustering at scale. Mechanical sequences with low entropy surface immediately.

03

Graph community (Leiden)

Dense, isolated transaction subgraphs are characteristic of internal-shuffle farms.

04

Temporal anomaly

Hour-of-day entropy, inter-tx autocorrelation, dormant-burst patterns. Humans are messy.

05

Cross-chain linking

Bridge events deterministically link wallet identities across 8 chains.

06

ML ensemble

LightGBM weighted by label confidence tier. Honest evaluation on T1+G1 holdout only.
