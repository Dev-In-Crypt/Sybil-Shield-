// which_methods_matter

FUNDING_CLUSTERING

DAO sybil attacks often use freshly-funded wallets from a single source — the highest-signal method here.

BEHAVIORAL_FINGERPRINT

Voting wallets created days before a contentious vote with no other activity = strong signal.

CROSS_CHAIN_LINKING

Entities holding voting tokens on multiple chains get linked back to one identity.

TEMPORAL_CORRELATION

Wallets voting within seconds of each other across many proposals are flagged as a coordinated bloc.
