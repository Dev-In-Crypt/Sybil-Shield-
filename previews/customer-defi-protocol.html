// context

A yield-farming protocol with per-wallet reward caps was bleeding rewards to a coordinator splitting capital across hundreds of fresh wallets.

// method

The /v1/score endpoint was wired into the deposit webhook. Any wallet scoring ≥70 had its reward escrowed pending review.

// outcome

Funding-source clustering attributed 380 wallets to a single funder. The escrowed rewards were redistributed to legitimate LPs after a 14-day appeal window.
